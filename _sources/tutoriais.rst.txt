Tutorials
=========

This section presents the training modules developed and delivered by the HARMONIZE Brazil INPE team during the project.

These tutorials demonstrate how to use the tools developed to access and manipulate EODCtHRS data, run queries, and visualize results, highlighting the technologies advanced by the team over the past years.
